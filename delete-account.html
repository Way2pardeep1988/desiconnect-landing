Delete Your DesiConnect Account

If you'd like to delete your DesiConnect account and remove all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

📩 Email Us

Send an email to contact.desiconnect@gmail.com with the subject “Delete My Account” and include the email address you used to register.

🗑️ What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your profile (email, photos, bio, etc.)
  • Your match history
  • Messages and voice notes

⏳ Data Deletion Timeline

We aim to delete all data within 7 days of your request. Some metadata may be temporarily retained for legal, fraud, or audit reasons.

If you have questions, feel free to contact us directly at the email above.
